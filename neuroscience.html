How it all started

This mathematical model aims to simulate processes of the Barchitecture system, from light activation to bacterial cell-cell adhesion to form 3D structures. The model has been used to direct and optimise our experimental approach.
It allowed us to select parameters for light intensity, predict expected timeframes needed for cell adhesion, and assess the theoretical limitations of our synbio system.
